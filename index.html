My top-3 movies of all time

1. 3-Idiots

I like this movie because it's like a complete movie for those who wants to follow their passion after secondary education.

2. MS Dhoni: An Untold Story

This movie has some of the great cinematic moments captured and its story is based on one of India's Successful Cricket Team Captain i.e; Mahendra Singh Dhoni

3. Sam Bahadur

This movie is a biopic of one of India's most influential personality and a Veteran Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw. This movie highlights his achievements during World War-II and other wars post independence.
